## Overdue
```tasks
not done
(due before today) OR (scheduled before today)
```

## Scheduled Today
```tasks
not done
(due today) OR (scheduled today)
```

## Scheduled Next 3 Days
```tasks
not done
((due after today) AND (due before in 4 days)) OR ((scheduled after today) AND (scheduled before in 4 days))
```

## No Date
```tasks
not done
no due date
no scheduled date
```
